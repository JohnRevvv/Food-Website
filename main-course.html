🍛 Main Course Food

Explore Japan’s most delicious and iconic meals

Popular Japanese Main Course Foods

🍜 Ramen

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

A comforting and savory noodle soup made with rich broth, wheat noodles, chashu pork, marinated egg, bamboo shoots, and green onions.

Where to Buy:
Ichiran, Ippudo, and local ramen shops across Japan.

🍣 Sushi

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Japan’s iconic culinary masterpiece featuring vinegared rice topped with premium fresh seafood like tuna, salmon, and shrimp.

Where to Buy:
Sushiro, Kura Sushi, and specialized sushi restaurants.

🍛 Katsu Curry

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Crispy breaded pork or chicken cutlet served over rice and covered with thick Japanese curry sauce.

Where to Buy:
CoCo Ichibanya and Japanese curry houses.

🍚 Donburi

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

A satisfying rice bowl topped with flavorful ingredients like beef, onions, eggs, and crispy pork cutlet.

Where to Buy:
Sukiya, Yoshinoya, and Matsuya branches.

🍜 Udon

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Thick chewy noodles served in a light dashi broth with toppings like scallions, tempura flakes, and tempura.

Where to Buy:
Marugame Seimen and traditional udon noodle shops.
