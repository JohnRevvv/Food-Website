🥢 Street Foods

Iconic Japanese street food experience

Popular Japanese Street Foods

🐙 Takoyaki

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Crunchy outside, gooey inside balls filled with octopus and topped with sauce.

Where to Buy: Street stalls and Gindaco shops.

🍢 Yakitori

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Grilled chicken skewers seasoned with salt or sweet soy glaze.

Where to Buy: Izakayas and street stalls.

🍗 Karaage

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Crispy Japanese fried chicken marinated in soy sauce and ginger.

Where to Buy: Convenience stores and festivals.

🥔 Korokke

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Golden fried potato croquette with crispy panko coating.

Where to Buy: Butcher shops and street stalls.

🦑 Ika Yaki

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Grilled whole squid brushed with sweet soy glaze over fire.

Where to Buy: Coastal markets and festivals.
