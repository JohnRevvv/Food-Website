🍡 Desserts

Sweet treats from Japan

Popular Japanese Desserts

🍡 Mochi

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Chewy rice cake often filled with red bean paste or cream and fruits.

Where to Buy: Wagashi shops and convenience stores.

🐟 Taiyaki

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Fish-shaped cake filled with custard, chocolate, or red bean paste.

Where to Buy: Street stalls and station shops.

🥞 Soufflé Pancakes

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Ultra fluffy pancakes that are soft, airy, and melt in your mouth.

Where to Buy: Trendy dessert cafés in Japan.

🍡 Dango

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 / 5)

Skewered rice dumplings glazed with sweet soy sauce or seasonal flavors.

Where to Buy: Wagashi shops and temples.

🍮 Purin

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 / 5)

Japanese custard pudding with smooth texture and caramel sauce topping.

Where to Buy: Convenience stores (7-Eleven, Lawson).
